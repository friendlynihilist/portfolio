About

I'm a philosopher and digital humanities researcher. My work explores semantic modeling, diagrams, digital annotation and knowledge representation in cultural heritage.

Selected Projects

  • Peirce Interprets Peirce Pipeline
    • A modular pipeline for layout segmentation and diagram recognition through VLMs in Peirce’s manuscripts.
  • GEkO – General Ekphrastic Ontology
    • An ontology for modeling ekphrastic relations between texts and visual artworks.
  • MLAO – Multi-Level Annotation Ontology
    • A semantic model for layered digital annotations in cultural heritage contexts.
  • Petrarca Online
    • A scholarly digital edition of Petrarch’s works realized with the Muruca framework, including a custom text viewer based on TEI Publisher.
  • Autografos Teatrales del Siglo de Oro
    • A digital archive of early modern Spanish theatrical manuscripts, based on the Muruca framework.
  • De Rerum Natura Digital Exhibition
    • An interactive semantic digital exhibition of Lucretius’s *De Rerum Natura* reception in contemporary art.
